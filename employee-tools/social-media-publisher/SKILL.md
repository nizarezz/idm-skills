# IDM Social Media Publisher

Create professional social media content that builds IDM's audience, drives engagement, and maintains clear separation between editorial, sponsored, and corporate content.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating social media posts for IDM channels
- Scheduling content across platforms
- Repurposing articles for social media
- Promoting events and conferences
- Sharing industry news and data

## Critical Rule: Content Separation

**Every social media post must be clearly categorized:**

| Category | Label | Description |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Editorial | (no label needed) | Original journalism, analysis, data |
| Sponsored | "Contenu Sponsorisé" / "محتوى ممول" / "Sponsored Content" | Paid partnerships |
| Corporate | "Communication IDM" / "تواصل IDIM" / "IDM Communication" | Company news |
| Advertising | "Publicité" / "إعلان" / "Advertisement" | Paid ads |

**Never present sponsored or advertising content as editorial.**

## Platform Guidelines

### LinkedIn (Primary Platform)

**Best for:** B2B, thought leadership, industry analysis
**Frequency:** 3-5 posts per week
**Language:** French (primary), English (international)

**Content Types:**
- Industry analysis and commentary
- Data visualizations
- Expert quotes and insights
- Event promotion
- Job listings (corporate only)

**Post Structure:**
1. Hook (1-2 sentences, create curiosity)
2. Key insight or finding
3. Supporting data or quote
4. Implication for Moroccan industry
5. Call to action or question
6. Hashtags (5-10 relevant)

**Example (Editorial):**
```markdown
Morocco's automotive sector produced 560,000 vehicles in H1 2026, up 8% year-over-year.

But the real story isn't the number — it's where those vehicles are going. European demand remains strong, but new markets in West Africa are emerging.

What does this mean for Morocco's industrial strategy? Our analysis: [link]

#Morocco #Automotive #Manufacturing #IndustrialPolicy #IndustrieDuMaroc
```

**Example (Sponsored):**
```markdown
Contenu Sponsorisé | محتوى ممول | Sponsored Content

[Company Name] announces new facility in Tangier Industrial Zone.

The investment of 500 million DH will create 300 jobs in automotive parts manufacturing.

Learn more: [link]

#Morocco #Investment #Automotive
```

### Twitter/X

**Best for:** Real-time news, industry community engagement
**Frequency:** 3-10 posts per day (including retweets)
**Language:** French, English, Arabic (varies)

**Content Types:**
- Breaking news and announcements
- Data points and statistics
- Industry commentary
- Thread posts (longer analysis)
- Retweets of industry partners

**Post Structure (Single Tweet):**
- Under 280 characters
- One key point or data
- Link if applicable
- 2-3 hashtags

**Thread Structure (5-10 tweets):**
1. Hook tweet (most surprising finding)
2. Data tweet (key statistic)
3. Context tweet (why this matters)
4. Quote tweet (expert perspective)
5. Implication tweet (what comes next)
6. CTA tweet (link to full article)

### Instagram

**Best for:** Visual content, behind-the-scenes, event coverage
**Frequency:** 1-2 posts + Stories daily
**Language:** French/Arabic (visual, less text-heavy)

**Content Types:**
- Factory and industrial photography
- Data visualization carousels
- Event coverage
- Behind-the-scenes at IDM
- Quote graphics

**Post Structure:**
- High-quality image or carousel
- Caption with key insight
- Hashtags (15-20 relevant)
- Tag relevant accounts

**Carousel Structure (8-12 slides):**
1. Cover: Title + hook
2. Problem/challenge
3. Key data slide 1
4. Key data slide 2
5. Expert quote
6. Comparison (before/after)
7. Implication
8. CTA

### Facebook

**Best for:** Community building, event promotion, longer updates
**Frequency:** 1-2 posts per day
**Language:** French, Arabic

**Content Types:**
- Article links with summaries
- Event promotion
- Photo galleries
- Community discussions
- Job postings (corporate only)

## Engagement Strategy

### Daily Routine (30 minutes)

1. **Monitor mentions** (5 minutes)
   - Respond to comments
   - Thank for shares
   - Address questions

2. **Engage with industry** (15 minutes)
   - Comment on 5-10 posts from industry accounts
   - Share relevant content with added insight
   - Join relevant conversations

3. **Create responses** (10 minutes)
   - Thank sources for interviews
   - Share reader feedback
   - Promote upcoming content

### Quality Comments

- Add new insight, not just "Great post!"
- Share a related experience or data point
- Ask a thoughtful follow-up question
- Respectfully disagree with nuance

### Building Relationships

- Identify 20-50 key industry accounts
- Consistently engage with their content
- Share their content with credit
- Eventually collaborate on content

## Analytics & Optimization

### Metrics That Matter

**Awareness:** Impressions, Reach, Follower growth
**Engagement:** Comments (higher value than likes), Shares, Saves
**Conversion:** Link clicks, Profile visits, DMs received

### Weekly Review

- Top 3 performing posts (why did they work?)
- Bottom 3 posts (what can you learn?)
- Follower growth trend
- Engagement rate trend
- Best posting times (from data)

### Optimization Actions

**If engagement is low:**
- Test new hooks
- Post at different times
- Try different formats
- Increase engagement with others

**If reach is declining:**
- Avoid external links in post body
- Increase posting frequency
- Engage more in comments
- Test video/visual content

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing any social media post:

### Content
- [ ] Categorized correctly (editorial/sponsored/corporate/advertising)
- [ ] Sponsored content clearly labeled
- [ ] All statistics sourced and dated
- [ ] No AI writing patterns (idm-ai-writing-detox)
- [ ] Appropriate tone for platform

### Style
- [ ] Follows IDM brand guide
- [ ] Headlines in sentence case
- [ ] Numbers formatted correctly
- [ ] Company names use official registered names

### Engagement
- [ ] Clear call to action
- [ ] Relevant hashtags
- [ ] Tagged relevant accounts
- [ ] Link works correctly

### Trilingual
- [ ] Language appropriate for audience
- [ ] Terminology matches IDM-TERMINOLOGY.md
- [ ] Company/government names consistent
- [ ] Translation preserves meaning
